About Me

My Journey

I'm a PhD Research Scholar in Entomology at Mississippi State University, where I study the genetic and environmental factors influencing viral dynamics in honey bee colonies. My journey from India to Mississippi has been driven by a passion for understanding pollinator health and developing strategies to protect these vital insects.

Under the guidance of Dr. Esmaeil Amiri, I'm investigating how we can select colonies that are resistant to viral infections, contributing to sustainable apiculture practices.
