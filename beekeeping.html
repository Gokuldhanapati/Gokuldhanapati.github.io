Beekeeping

Beekeeping, or apiculture, bridges the gap between academic research and practical conservation. Understanding hive management practices provides essential context for laboratory research on bee health, disease resistance, and pollinator decline.

This section documents practical beekeeping knowledge, seasonal hive management protocols, and observations from field work that inform my molecular research.

More notes will be added as research progresses.
