Hello, I'm

Gokul Dhanapati

PhD Scholar | Entomology | Honey Bee Health

I am a researcher studying honey bees and the viruses that affect their health. My work focuses on understanding how viral infections impact bees and contribute to colony mortality. By combining laboratory research and field observations, I aim to better understand the factors that influence bee survival and colony health. Through this research, I hope to contribute to improving honey bee health and supporting the pollinators that are essential for ecosystems and agriculture.
