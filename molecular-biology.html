Molecular Biology

Molecular biology provides the foundational toolkit for understanding life at its most fundamental level. From gene expression and protein interactions to epigenetic regulation, these mechanisms underpin every biological process I study.

This section collects learning notes on techniques such as RNA-seq, qPCR, CRISPR gene editing, and protein crystallography — with an emphasis on their application to entomological research.

More notes will be added as research progresses.
