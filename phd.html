Research Focus

Genetic and Environmental Drivers of Viral Resistance in Honey Bee Colonies

My doctoral research investigates the complex interplay between genetic factors and environmental conditions that influence viral resistance mechanisms in honey bee populations. Using advanced molecular techniques and statistical modeling, I aim to identify key biomarkers and resistance pathways.
